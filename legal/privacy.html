We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, list a service, or make a booking.

Personal Information

  • Event details and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our platform services

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only as described in this policy:

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights
